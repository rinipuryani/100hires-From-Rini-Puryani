# Expert Sources

Topic: Modern B2B SaaS growth across positioning, demand generation, product-led growth, conversion, founder-led go-to-market, and audience research.

Collection date started: 2026-07-05

This research focuses on practitioners whose public content can support a future B2B SaaS growth playbook. I prioritized people who actively build, advise, invest in, or interview SaaS operators, not only writers who discuss growth from the outside.

## Selection Criteria

- Practitioner credibility: the expert has built, operated, advised, invested in, or repeatedly worked with SaaS companies.
- Content usefulness: the expert publishes frameworks, interviews, examples, or tactical material that can become playbook sections.
- Topic coverage: the group covers positioning, demand generation, product-led growth, onboarding, retention, conversion, founder-led GTM, scaling, and audience research.
- Source availability: the expert has public YouTube, podcast, LinkedIn, Instagram, newsletter, or other materials that can be collected.

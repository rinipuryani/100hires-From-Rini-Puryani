### Image Extracted Text: The Hook, Line, & Sinker Framework

**Goal**
* **Hook:** Grabs attention; makes a promise of content to come; entices the visitor to further engage
* **Line:** Delivers on the promise; triggers memorable associations; earns an emotional reaction
* **Sinker:** Makes the content stay with the consumer after they leave; inspires sharing and engagement

**Content Element(s)**
* **Hook:** Title / Headline, First 1-2 ¶s, Preview/featured image, First 30 secs of video/audio
* **Line:** The "meat" of the post/article, Data, charts, visuals, interactive, etc., Post-intro video/audio content
* **Sinker:** The most resonant elements of can live anywhere, but the piece's end should remind readers/viewers/listeners of them (i.e. a powerful end-quote, a last graphic, a gut-punching conclusion).

## Research Playbook: Expert Selection & Data Collection

### What We Collected
We have gathered substantial material from 10 top-tier B2B marketing and growth experts. The repository is organized into:
- `/research/youtube-transcripts/` - Detailed YouTube transcripts of keynotes, masterclasses, and interviews. We explicitly filtered out videos under 10 minutes to ensure deep, actionable content.
- `/research/linkedin-posts/` - High-performing LinkedIn posts and frameworks from each expert.
- `/research/other/` - Newsletters, blogs, and website content (e.g., Substack, Refine Labs, SaaStr).
- `/research/sources.md` - Master list of all experts with their background, specific topic focus, and index of collected materials.

### Why We Chose These Experts
We prioritized **practitioners over theorists**. Instead of relying on generic SEO articles, we selected industry leaders who are actively executing what they teach in the real world:
1. **April Dunford**: The undeniable authority on Product Positioning.
2. **Chris Walker**: Redefined Demand Generation and Dark Social for modern B2B SaaS.
3. **Dave Gerhardt**: Pioneered founder-led content marketing and brand building.
4. **Peep Laja**: Master of messaging and conversion optimization through category design.
5. **Wes Bush**: A leading voice and architect of the Product-Led Growth (PLG) movement.
6. **Elena Verna**: Premier advisor on B2B growth loops and monetization.
7. **Jason Lemkin**: The go-to expert for SaaS scaling and Go-To-Market strategy via SaaStr.
8. **TK Kader**: Practical frameworks for Founder-led GTM and building pipeline.
9. **Harry Stebbings**: Aggregates elite-level executive strategies via 20VC.
10. **Rand Fishkin**: Champion of zero-click marketing and audience-first SEO strategies.

These specific voices ensure that our playbook is built upon proven, battle-tested methodologies rather than basic Google search results.
